# Lab 19: GRC201 Module

**Course:** GRC201 - Incident Management & Business Continuity  
**Week:** 19  
**Duration:** 5 days  
**Level:** Advanced  
**Prerequisites:** Phase 1 completion

## Lab Overview

This lab focuses on incident response and business continuity planning. Participants will develop IR plans, create BCP/DRP documentation, and conduct tabletop exercises.

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this lab, you will be able to:

- Develop comprehensive incident response plans
- Create business continuity and disaster recovery plans
- Conduct incident response tabletop exercises
- Document forensic procedures
- Establish crisis communication protocols

## Required Resources

- Incident response template (templates/incident_response_template.md)
- BCP/DRP template
- Sample incident scenarios
- Lab report template

## Lab Tasks

### Task 1: Incident Response Planning

Develop comprehensive IR plan including:
- IR team structure and roles
- Incident classification
- Response procedures
- Communication protocols
- Recovery procedures

### Task 2: Business Continuity Planning

Create BCP/DRP documentation:
- Business impact analysis
- Recovery objectives (RTO/RPO)
- Recovery procedures
- Testing schedule
- Maintenance procedures

### Task 3: Incident Simulation

Conduct tabletop exercise:
- Scenario development
- Team participation
- Response evaluation
- Finding documentation
- Improvement recommendations

## Assessment Criteria

- Plan completeness (25%)
- Procedure clarity (25%)
- Tabletop execution (20%)
- Documentation quality (20%)
- Recommendations (10%)

## Submission Requirements

Submit by Friday 11:59 PM:

1. Lab Report
2. IR Plan documentation
3. BCP/DRP documentation
4. Tabletop exercise results
5. Improvement recommendations
