# GRC Engineering Programme - 52 Week Schedule

## Overview

This document provides the complete 52-week schedule for the ICDFA Certified GRC Engineer (CGRCE) programme, including all labs, milestones, and key dates.

## Phase I: Compliance & Governance Foundations (Weeks 1-18)

### GRC101 - Introduction to GRC & Technical Landscape (Weeks 1-4)

| Week | Lab | Title | Focus | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----|-------|-------|------------------|
| 1 | Lab 1 | Linux for GRC Operations | Linux fundamentals, file system, logs | Lab report, system exploration evidence |
| 2 | Lab 2 | Linux Scripting for Compliance | Bash scripting, automation | Compliance automation script, report |
| 3 | Lab 3 | Understanding GRC Frameworks | NIST CSF, control mapping | Framework mapping document |
| 4 | Lab 4 | Risk & Compliance Basics | Risk assessment fundamentals | Risk register, assessment report |

**Milestone:** End of Week 4 - GRC101 Module Complete

### GRC102 - Information Security Governance (Weeks 5-9)

| Week | Lab | Title | Focus | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----|-------|-------|------------------|
| 5 | Lab 5 | Policy Development Lab | Policy writing, ISO 27001 alignment | Policy documents (AUP, Password Policy) |
| 6 | Lab 6 | Governance Roles Mapping | RACI matrix, governance structure | RACI matrix, governance architecture |
| 7 | Lab 7 | Security Control Monitoring | Monitoring checklists, log evaluation | Monitoring dashboard, findings |
| 8 | Lab 8 | Internal Audit Simulation | Mock audit, evidence collection | Audit findings report |
| 9 | Lab 9 | Governance Case Study | Real-world analysis, gap identification | Case resolution report |

**Milestone:** End of Week 9 - GRC102 Module Complete

### GRC103 - Risk Assessment & Management (Weeks 10-14)

| Week | Lab | Title | Focus | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----|-------|-------|------------------|
| 10 | Lab 10 | Threat Modelling Lab | STRIDE methodology, data flow diagrams | Threat model report, DFDs |
| 11 | Lab 11 | Quantitative Risk Calculation | ALE, SLE, ARO calculations | Risk scoring workbook |
| 12 | Lab 12 | Qualitative Risk Workshop | Heatmaps, likelihood-impact analysis | Heatmap, qualitative model |
| 13 | Lab 13 | Risk Treatment Strategy | Control selection, risk acceptance | Treatment plan, acceptance memo |
| 14 | Lab 14 | Enterprise Risk Register | Corporate risk register development | Complete risk register |

**Milestone:** End of Week 14 - GRC103 Module Complete

### GRC104 - Compliance & Legal Requirements (Weeks 15-18)

| Week | Lab | Title | Focus | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----|-------|-------|------------------|
| 15 | Lab 15 | Framework Mapping Lab | ISO 27001, NIST, CIS alignment | Controls alignment table |
| 16 | Lab 16 | Data Privacy Assessment | GDPR/NDPA gap assessment | Privacy compliance checklist |
| 17 | Lab 17 | Legal Impact Simulation | Regulatory non-compliance evaluation | Legal impact brief |
| 18 | Lab 18 | Compliance Audit Preparation | Evidence gathering, audit scheduling | Full audit prep package |

**Milestone:** End of Week 18 - Phase I Complete, Phase I Examination

| Assessment | Week | Type | Weight |
|------------|------|------|--------|

| Phase II Exam | 35 | Practical | 13% |

| Capstone Project | 44-47 | Practical | 20% |

### Submission Deadlines

All lab submissions are due by **11:59 PM on the Friday of each week** unless otherwise specified.

Late submissions will incur a 10% penalty per day, with a maximum of 3 days late submission allowed.

## Notes and Tips

1. **Plan Ahead:** Review the schedule at the beginning of each month
2. **Stay on Track:** Complete labs by the Friday deadline each week
3. **Prepare for Exams:** Begin exam preparation 2 weeks before the exam date
4. **Capstone Planning:** Start thinking about capstone topics from Week 36
5. **Document Progress:** Keep records of all completed labs and assessments
6. **Seek Support:** Contact instructors if you fall behind schedule
